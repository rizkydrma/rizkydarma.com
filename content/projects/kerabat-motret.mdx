## Why I create this project?

This project website is created to share information about the photography services that my friends and I offer. This is because potential users of our services often want to see prices, catalogs, and other information without needing to contact us. Information regarding our photography services can already be accessed by prospective customers.

## Project Goals

1. Displaying price information and benefits.
2. Showcasing our portfolio.
3. Featuring the individuals involved.
4. Presenting other relevant information.

## Tech Stack Used

In this project, I am using React.js for development, and for styling, I'm utilizing Scss.
